Welcome to
Maleda Handcraft

The Maleda Handmade Craft Difference.

What sets us apart is our dedication to authenticity and quality. Each item in our collection is meticulously crafted by hand with precision and attention to detail. Our close collaboration with highly skilled artisans guarantees that every piece we create narrates a distinct story of artistry and cultural legacy.

Core Values

  • Authenticity: Keeping things real with genuine craftsmanship.
  • Connection: Bringing artisans and customers closer together.
  • Creativity: Embracing innovation in artisanal work.
  • Sustainability: Going green for a better future.
  • Community: Building a supportive handmade community.
  • Quality: Ensuring top-notch handmade products.
  • Empowerment: Helping artisans shine and grow.
  • Appreciation: Cherishing the stories behind each creation.
  • Cultural Preservation: Safeguarding traditional craftsmanship.
  • Customer Centricity: Putting customers first for a seamless experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to close the gap that exists between modern living and traditional workmanship. We believe that handmade goods have the ability to evoke feelings, tell stories, and make enduring memories. Through the selection of a wide variety of handcrafted items, we aim to connect artisans with appreciative people who value uniqueness in a hectic society.

Our Artisan

Get to know the creative people that run Maleda! Our artisans offer a variety of influences and techniques to our creative community. In addition to purchasing a unique item, you're also helping to sustain the livelihoods of dedicated artists who put their all into each and every creation by shopping with us.

Objectives

  • Curate a diverse selection of high-quality handmade products
  • Showcase the talent and craftsmanship of artisans worldwide
  • Create a platform for artists to reach a wider audience
  • Promote sustainable and ethical practices in handmade goods

Goals

  • Foster a community supporting handmade products
  • Provide a seamless shopping experience
  • Expand artisan networks for collaborations.
  • Preserve traditional craftsmanship and heritage
